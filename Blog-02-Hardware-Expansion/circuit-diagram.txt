Component Details:
==================

LEDs:
- Anode (long leg, +) connects to GPIO pin
- Cathode (short leg, -) connects to resistor

Resistors:
- 220Ω (Red-Red-Brown-Gold)
- One end to LED cathode
- Other end to ground rail

Breadboard:
- Blue rail = Ground (GND)
- Red rail = Not used in this project
- Terminal strips connect rows (a-e, f-j)

Pin Assignments:
================
GPIO 23 → Red LED
GPIO 22 → Yellow LED
GPIO 21 → Green LED
GPIO 19 → Blue LED
GND → Breadboard ground rail


Safety Notes:
=============
✓ Always use current-limiting resistors
✓ Check LED polarity before powering
✓ ESP32 GPIO max current: 12mA (we use ~10mA)
✓ Total GPIO current limit: 40mA (we use ~40mA max)
✓ Never connect LED directly to GPIO without resistor!


Wiring Checklist:
=================
□ ESP32 GND connected to breadboard GND rail
□ All 4 LEDs inserted with correct polarity
□ All 4 resistors connected from LED cathodes to GND
□ All 4 GPIO wires connected to LED anodes
□ No short circuits between adjacent pins
□ All connections firm and secure
